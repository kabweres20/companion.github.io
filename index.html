Glossary of Cosmetic Procedures

Abdominoplasty: A surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the abdomen and to tighten the muscles of the abdominal wall.
Arm Lift (Brachioplasty): A surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the upper arms.
Arthritis: Treatment options to manage and alleviate the symptoms of arthritis.

Blepharoplasty (Eyelid Surgery): A surgical procedure to improve the appearance of the eyelids.
Blepharospasm: Treatment for involuntary tight closure of the eyelids.
Biopsies: A medical test involving the extraction of sample cells or tissues for examination.
Birthmarks: Treatment options to lighten or remove birthmarks.
Botox: A non-surgical procedure to reduce or eliminate facial lines and wrinkles.
Bra Roll Excision: A surgical procedure to remove excess fat rolls from the back.
Brachial Plexus Injury/Nerve Reconstruction: Surgical repair of damaged nerves in the brachial plexus.
Breast Augmentation: A surgical procedure to increase breast size using implants.
Breast Cancer Reconstruction: Reconstruction of the breast following mastectomy.
Breast Implants: Insertion of implants to enhance breast size and shape.
Breast Lift: A surgical procedure to raise and firm the breasts.
Breast Reconstruction with Tissue Expanders: Reconstruction using tissue expanders to create space for implants.
Breast Reduction: A surgical procedure to reduce the size of the breasts.
Brow Lift: A surgical procedure to raise the eyebrows and reduce forehead wrinkles.

Carpal Tunnel: Surgical treatment for carpal tunnel syndrome to relieve pressure on the median nerve.
Cheek Implants (Malar Augmentation): Insertion of implants to enhance the cheekbones.
Chemical Peels: A technique to improve skin texture and tone using a chemical solution.
Chest Feminization Surgery: Surgical procedures to create a more feminine chest contour.
Chest Masculinization Surgery: Surgical procedures to create a more masculine chest contour.
Chin Augmentation (Mentoplasty): A surgical procedure to enhance the chin's size and shape.
Circumferential Body Lift: A comprehensive procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the abdomen, buttocks, and thighs.
Cleft Lip & Palate Repair: Surgical correction of cleft lip and/or palate.
Cosmetic Fillers: Injections to fill wrinkles and add volume to the face.
Cranial Defect Repair: Surgical repair of cranial bone defects.

Deviated Septum: Surgical correction of a deviated septum to improve breathing.
DIEP/TRAM Flap: Breast reconstruction using the patient's own tissue from the lower abdomen.
Dupuytren's Contracture: Surgical treatment to release thickened tissue in the palm and fingers.
DysportÂ®: An injection treatment to reduce facial wrinkles.

Ear Lobe Repair: Surgical repair of torn or stretched earlobes.
Ear Pinning Surgery: A surgical procedure to correct prominent ears.
Electrolysis Hair Removal: A method of permanent hair removal using electrical currents.
Embolization of Vascular Malformations: A procedure to block abnormal blood vessels.
Endovenous Ablation: A treatment for varicose veins using heat to close off veins.
Excessive Sweating: Treatment options to manage hyperhidrosis (excessive sweating).
Eye Alignment: Surgical correction of eye misalignment (strabismus).
Eye Lift Surgery: A surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the upper and lower eyelids.
Eyelid Lift (Blepharoplasty): A surgical procedure to improve the appearance of the eyelids.
Eyelid Spasm: Treatment for involuntary eyelid muscle contractions.

Facelift: A surgical procedure to reduce wrinkles and sagging skin on the face and neck.
Facial Feminization Surgery: Surgical procedures to create a more feminine facial appearance.
Facial Fracture: Surgical treatment to repair broken facial bones.
Facial Palsy (re-animation): Surgical procedures to restore movement to a paralyzed face.
Facial Sculpting: Procedures to enhance and contour the face.
Facials: A spa treatment to improve skin health and appearance.
Fat Transfer: A procedure to move fat from one part of the body to another.
Fillers: Injections to fill wrinkles and add volume to the face.
Finger/Hand Fracture: Surgical treatment to repair broken bones in the fingers or hand.
Flap Reconstruction: Use of tissue flaps to reconstruct areas of the body.
Forehead Lift: A surgical procedure to raise the eyebrows and reduce forehead wrinkles.

Ganglion Excision: Surgical removal of a ganglion cyst.
Gender Affirming Surgery: Surgical procedures to align physical appearance with gender identity.
Gynecomastia (male breast reduction): A surgical procedure to reduce the size of male breasts.

Hidradenitis Suppurativa: Treatment options for this chronic skin condition causing small, painful lumps under the skin.
Hyperhydrosis: Treatment options for excessive sweating.

Inverted Nipple Correction: A surgical procedure to correct inverted nipples.

Labiaplasty: A surgical procedure to reduce the size of the labia minora.
Laser Hair Removal: A non-invasive procedure to remove unwanted hair using laser technology.
Laser Leg Vein Removal: A procedure to remove spider veins and small varicose veins on the legs using laser.
Laser Scar Removal: A procedure to reduce the appearance of scars using laser technology.
Laser Skin Rejuvenation: A procedure to improve skin texture, tone, and appearance using laser technology.
Laser Tattoo Removal: A procedure to remove unwanted tattoos using laser technology.
Laser Vision Correction: A procedure to correct vision problems using laser technology.
Liposuction: A surgical procedure to remove excess fat deposits and contour the body.

Male Breast Reduction: A surgical procedure to reduce the size of male breasts.
Mandible Repair: Surgical repair of a fractured or injured jawbone.
Microphlebectomy: A minimally invasive procedure to remove varicose veins.
Mohs Surgery and Reconstruction: A surgical technique to remove skin cancer with minimal damage to surrounding tissue.
Mole Removal: A procedure to remove moles for cosmetic or medical reasons.
Mommy Makeover: A combination of cosmetic procedures to restore a woman's pre-pregnancy body.

NAM Device: A device used to mold and reshape the gums, lips, and nostrils before cleft lip and palate surgery.
Nasal Fracture: Surgical treatment to repair a broken nose.
Nasal Obstruction: Surgical correction of blocked nasal passages.
Neurofibroma Excision (Electrodessication): Removal of benign nerve sheath tumors.
Neurofibromatosis: Treatment options for this genetic disorder causing tumors to form on nerve tissue.
Neuroma: Surgical removal of a benign nerve tumor.
Nipple/Areola Reconstruction: Reconstruction of the nipple and areola after breast surgery.
Nipple Tattooing: Cosmetic tattooing to create the appearance of a nipple and areola.

Percutaneous Radiofrequency Closure: A minimally invasive procedure to close varicose veins.
Phalloplasty: Surgical construction or reconstruction of a penis.
Pilonidal Cyst: Surgical removal of a cyst near the tailbone.
Polydactyly / Syndactyly Repair: Surgical correction of extra fingers or toes (polydactyly) or fused fingers or toes (syndactyly).
Ptosis (drooping eyelid): Surgical correction of drooping upper eyelids.

Redness, Rosacea: Treatment options to reduce facial redness and manage rosacea symptoms.
Rhinoplasty: A surgical procedure to reshape the nose for cosmetic or functional reasons.

Scar Revision: Surgical procedures to improve the appearance of scars.
Scar Removal: Techniques to reduce or remove scars.
Sclerotherapy: A procedure to treat spider veins and varicose veins by injecting a solution that causes them to collapse and fade.
Skin Resurfacing: Procedures to improve skin texture, tone, and appearance.
Skin Tag Removal: Removal of small, benign skin growths.
Spider Veins: Treatment options to reduce or remove small, visible veins.
Steroid Injection: Injections to reduce inflammation and pain in various conditions.
Strabismus: Surgical correction of eye misalignment.
Surgical Revision: Additional surgery to improve or correct the outcome of a previous procedure.

Tattoo Removal: Techniques to remove unwanted tattoos.
Thigh Lift: A surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the thighs.
Top Surgery: Surgical procedures to remove breast tissue for a more masculine chest contour.
Transgender Body Contouring: Procedures to alter body shape to align with gender identity.
Transgender Surgical Procedures: Various surgical procedures to align physical appearance with gender identity.
Trigger Finger: Surgical treatment to release the tendon causing trigger finger.
Tummy Tuck: A surgical procedure to remove excess skin and fat from the abdomen and to tighten the muscles of the abdominal wall.

Varicose Veins: Treatment options to reduce or remove enlarged veins.
Vascular Malformations: Treatment options for abnormal blood vessel formations.
Veins: General treatments for vein conditions.

Waxing: A method of temporary hair removal using wax.
